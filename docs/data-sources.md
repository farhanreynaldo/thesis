# Data Sources and Documentation

## Electoral Data

### State-Level Presidential Election Returns (1976-2020)
- Source: MIT Election Data and Science Lab
- Coverage: Comprehensive constituency-level returns for U.S. presidential elections spanning over four decades
- Access: Available through Harvard Dataverse
- Link: [MIT Election Lab State-Level Returns](https://dataverse.harvard.edu/dataset.xhtml?persistentId=doi:10.7910/DVN/42MVDX)

### County-Level Presidential Election Returns (2000-2020)
- Source: MIT Election Data and Science Lab
- Coverage: Detailed county-by-county electoral returns for six presidential election cycles
- Access: Available through Harvard Dataverse
- Link: [MIT Election Lab County-Level Returns](https://dataverse.harvard.edu/dataset.xhtml?persistentId=doi:10.7910/DVN/VOQCHQ)

## Survey Data

### Cooperative Congressional Election Study (2006-2023)
- Source: Harvard Dataverse
- Coverage: Comprehensive survey data spanning 17 years of electoral behavior and public opinion
- Significance: One of the largest and most detailed political surveys in the United States
- Link: [CCES Dataset](https://dataverse.harvard.edu/dataset.xhtml?persistentId=doi%3A10.7910/DVN/II2DB6)

## Demographic Data

### Population Statistics
- Source: IPUMS-USA
- Access Method: Available through the {ipumsr} package
- Coverage: Detailed population size data at various geographic levels
- Features: Integrated Public Use Microdata Series providing comprehensive demographic information
- Link: [IPUMS-USA Database](https://usa.ipums.org/usa/)

### American Community Survey (ACS) Data
- Source: U.S. Census Bureau
- Access Method: Collected via tidycensus R package
- Data Type: ACS 1-Year estimates
- Variables: 
  - Median household income
  - Total population
- Features: Provides state-level household median income and total population across states

### Urban-Rural Classification
- Source: Office of Management and Budget (OMB)
- Version: 2023 definition
- Purpose: Standardized classification of urban and rural areas
- Documentation: Rural-Urban Continuum Codes
- Links:
  - [ERS USDA Documentation](https://www.ers.usda.gov/data-products/rural-urban-continuum-codes/)
  - [Detailed Classification Codes](https://www.ers.usda.gov/webdocs/DataFiles/53251/ruralurbancodes2013.xls?v=4020.1)

*Note: All datasets are publicly accessible through their respective repositories and can be used for research purposes with appropriate citation.*
